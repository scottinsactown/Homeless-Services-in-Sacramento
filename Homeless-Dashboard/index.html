A Homeless Management Information Systems (HMIS) is a database used to confidentially aggregate data on homeless populations served across the United States. Local homeless assistance providers use HMIS to coordinate care, manage their operations, and better serve their clients. The most recent data for Sacramento County was made available via a public records request and is shared here to provde a glimpse into the demand for homlessness related services, and how many who enroll for services secure permanent housing. Raw data is housed by Code for Sacramento.
